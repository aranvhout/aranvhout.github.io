Welcome

Hi, my name is Aran van Hout. I am currently working as a research assistant at the LCD-lab, where I contribute to the OMMS project. In this project, I am developing a program that generates matrices for assessing fluid reasoning. Learn more about the research here.

Beyond coding, I am fascinated by neuro-evolution and translational research. Studying brain differences across species to better understand our neuroecological niche is just super cool! I am also passionate about developmental research, working with big data, and exploring neuroimaging techniques.

Future Endeavours

Currently, I am on the look-out for a PhD related to the fields of neuroecology, developmental research, or neuroimaging! These are my dream subjects, and it would be amazing to further develop myself in these areas.
